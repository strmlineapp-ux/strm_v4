# Strm_ User Manual (2026)


Welcome to Strm_, your team's central hub for managing tasks, scheduling events, and coordinating production workflows. This guide will walk you through the key features of the application.


---


## 1. Getting Started


### 1.1 Requesting Access
To begin using Strm_, you must first be invited or request access to your company's specific workspace from an administrator.
1.  Navigate to your company's unique Strm_ URL (e.g., `your-company.strm.app`).
2.  Click the "Sign in with Google" button.
3.  If you have not been pre-approved, your account will be created in a **'Viewer'** (pending) state.
4.  An administrator will review your request. You will receive an email notification once your access is upgraded to **'Full'**.


### 1.2 Signing In
Once your access is approved:
1.  Navigate to your company's **Strm_ login** page.
2.  Click "Sign in with Google."
3.  Upon successful authentication, you will be taken to your default calendar view.


---


## 2. Navigating the App


The main navigation is located in the sidebar on the left. Hover over any icon to see its name via tooltip.


- **Main Navigation**: Quick access to core features like the Calendar, Overview, and Tasks.
- **Team Management**: If you manage a team, links to your team management pages appear here.
- **Admin Pages**: If you are a System Admin, you will see the Admin control panel link.
- **User Profile**: Located at the bottom of the sidebar. Access your profile, notifications, and the "View as" feature. The **Settings** page is accessed exclusively from this menu.


---


## 3. Dashboard Overview


The **Overview** page provides a high-level summary of activity. This is a "seamless" page with no visible header. You will find cards for **Active Tasks**, **Tasks Due Soon**, and **Completed Tasks**, along with a list of your most recent assignments.


---


## 4. Calendar


The calendar is the visual heart of Strm_, designed for complex production scheduling.


### 4.1 Creating Events
- Click the **(+)** icon in the header to open the new event form.
- **Easy Booking**: Enable this in your preferences to create events simply by clicking any empty time slot in the Day, Week, or Production Schedule views. The time and date will pre-fill automatically.


### 4.2 Calendar Views
- **Month/Week**: Traditional overviews.
- **Day**: Detailed hourly view. Can be toggled between horizontal or vertical axes.
- **Production Schedule**: A specialized 2026 view organizing events by **Location** for the entire week.
- **Lunch Break**: Look for the subtle diagonal pattern between 12:00 and 14:30—a visual reminder to keep that time clear.


---


## 5. Tasks


The **Tasks** page helps you track deliverables.
- **Grouping**: Tasks are grouped by status (e.g., *In Progress*, *Awaiting Review*).
- **New Task**: Click the `add_circle` button located directly below the tabs.
- **Management**: Use the "more" menu (`...`) on any task row to edit details or delete the entry.


---


## 6. Notifications


The **Notifications** page manages system alerts and access requests.
- **Admin Actions**: Administrators can approve or reject new user requests directly from this list.
- **Unread Badge**: A numerical badge on the sidebar icon indicates how many alerts require your attention.


---


## 7. Settings & Preferences


Access **"Account Settings"** by clicking your avatar in the bottom-left. This page is "seamless" (no header), and the search bar is automatically focused for finding colleagues.


Your settings are managed in a **Compact Preferences Row** on your user card. **To reset a preference to default, hold your Modifier Key (e.g., Shift) and click the icon.**


- **Custom Primary Color**: Use the palette icon to override the system theme with your own hex color.
- **Theme Toggle**: Switch between `light` and `dark` modes instantly via the sun/moon icon.
- **Weights & Grades**: Adjust the sliders to change global font weight and icon "grade" (boldness).
- **Easy Booking**: Toggle the click-to-create feature on or off.
- **Modifier Key**: Choose which key (`Shift`, `Alt`, `Ctrl`, or `Meta`) triggers secondary reset actions.


---


## 8. Management (Admin & Manager Roles)


### 8.1 Admin (`/dashboard/admin`)
The central control for the Strm_ environment.
- **User Management**: Drag a user to the "Admins" list to promote them. This requires a **2FA code** for security.
- **Pre-approval**: Use the `add_circle` button on the Users card to invite new emails for instant access.
- **Page Configuration**: Reorder the app’s sidebar by dragging pages in the masonry grid. **Pinned** pages (like Settings) cannot be moved.


### 8.2 Team Management
- **Members Tab**: Drag members between the "Members" and "Team Admins" lists.
- **Badge Assignments**: To move a badge from one person to another, drag the badge icon from the source member's card and drop it onto the target.
- **Sharing Collections**: Drag a Badge Collection card to the "Shared" side panel to make it available to other teams.
- **Presets**: Create reusable event templates. Click the text directly to use the **Inline Editor** for name changes.


### 8.3 "View As" Feature (Admin Only)
Test configurations by viewing the app as another user. Select "View as" from your profile menu. A top banner will remind you that you are in a simulated view; return to your own profile via the same menu.
